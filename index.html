Beyond The Friendship Bracelet

There are two types of friendship bracelets, normal and alpha. This website is dedicated to my alpha creations, beyond just bracelets. I also make patches for keychains or any type of alpha pattern.

*All crafts are made with six-strand divisible Embroidery Floss, 100% cotton. Colors are by LE PAON and may appear different on the web.

Normal

Normal patterns are tied downwards. They can use anywhere from only 1 knot to all 4 knots. When you are making a normal bracelet, the strings change order every time you tie a knot. This means that the "working" strings and "background" strings will also change every so often. Polpular patterns include:

  • Chinese Staircase
  • Candy Stripe
  • Chevron

Alpha

Alpha bracelets are tied in straight horizontal rows. The only knots used in Alpha bracelets are the forward knot and the backward knot. You alternate entire rows of these 2 knots. So, if your first row is only using a forward knot, then your second row is only using a backward knot, and so on. You substitute in different colors as needed. The cool thing about Alphas is that anything you can draw in a grid, you can make into a bracelet.

For more information about the different types of bracelets or to search for patterns, visit FriendshipBracelets.net or BraceletBook.com
